How It Works:

  1. Register Your Store: Sign up with your store’s basic details — no complicated forms or tech setup required.
  2. Send Product Details via WhatsApp: Share product details (product, color, size, stock, price) via WhatsApp. Our bot handles the rest!
  3. Instant Updates to the Wear60 App: Your products are added to the Wear60 app for customers to browse and buy.
  4. Start Selling & Delivering: Customers view your listings and order. We handle inventory updates in real time.
